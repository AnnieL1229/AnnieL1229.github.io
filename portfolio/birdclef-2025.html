BirdCLEF 2025 — Multi-Label Audio Classification

Identifying 206 bird species from noisy audio recordings.

Audio ML Multi-Label Classification Deep Learning Model Ensembling Data Augmentation

Overview

Built a two-stage system for the BirdCLEF 2025 challenge to detect 206 animal species from one-minute field recordings captured in Colombia. The pipeline converts raw audio into visual spectrograms and trains an image classifier (EfficientNet-B0) alongside a Sound Event Detection branch that improves sensitivity to brief or infrequent calls.

Training incorporated window sampling, data augmentation, and test-time averaging, with predictions from both models fused and smoothed across adjacent time segments.

Key result: The final system achieved a public leaderboard AUC of 0.851.
