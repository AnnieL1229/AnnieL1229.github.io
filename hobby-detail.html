Analog Photography

35mm Film / Urban / B&W

The Process

Photography allows me to step away from the digital noise and focus on composition and lighting. I shoot primarily on Ilford HP5 Plus black and white film.

The limitation of having only 36 exposures forces a deliberate slowness that contrasts with the high speed of data science. I develop my own film at home, enjoying the tactile chemistry of the process.
