About

I’m Annie Liu, a Master of Business Analytics candidate at MIT Sloan working at the intersection of machine learning, optimization, and product strategy. I build decision-ready analytical systems that translate messy, real-world data into clear business insight—from fraud screening and cold-start recommendation to demand forecasting and multimodal AI. My interests span consumer behavior, user experience analytics, and AI-driven product strategy.

Currently, I’m developing a multimodal AI agent with Kohler through the Generative AI Lab, building an intelligent “digital interior designer” that integrates images, product data, and knowledge retrieval. For my Capstone project, I’m partnering with Coca-Cola Southwest Beverages to design a promotion-aware demand forecasting model that supports commercial planning and inventory strategy.

Outside of analytics, I’m a long-time storyteller. I share my journey as a woman in STEM on Douyin and Rednote, where I’ve built a community of 25K followers. It has become a meaningful way for me to communicate ideas, reflect on growth, and connect with a global audience.
